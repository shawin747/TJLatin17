Helpful Links

Chapter Minutes

Google Drive folder of all of our minutes from our meetings.

Whitaker's Words

Best Latin dictionary out there! Can translate from English to Latin and vice versa.

Wiktionary

Not just for Latin! Has detailed etymology and extra info for every word you can imagine. There are also translations of many Latin works on Wiktionary, such as Catullus's poems.

History of the Roman Empire

Fantastic resource for learning about Roman History. Detailed summaries of different historical periods as well as pictures and maps of Rome.

Virginia JCL

Virginia JCL Website with information about Certamen, NJCL Convention, forms, etc.

Virginia Senior Classical League

Info on ways to stay involved with the JCL after high school! The Virginia Senior Classical League is a group of college students who work to support the Virginia Junior Classical League. They run Kick-Off Certamen as well as State Finals for Certamen.

Classical Association of Virginia

Check out information about the Classical Association of Virginia! They have tests and tournaments and news about classics-related events.

National Junior Classical League

Tons of information here about NJCL Convention and the activities of the NJCL. There are also lots of really helpful online exam resources to be found here as well!
