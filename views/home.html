Vergil's Birthday

Our AP Latin students commemorate Vergil's birthday by re-enacting his naming ceremony and celebrating with lots of fun, food, and cake!

Comissatio

Our Latin 3's start off their year by getting married (well, kind of)! They learn about the comissatio and celebrate the festivities a traditional high-class Roman marriage entails.

Myth Pumpkins

The entire TJ Latin club decorates spooky, realistic (although, some seem a little abstract), and creative myth pumpkins to celebrate Halloween!
