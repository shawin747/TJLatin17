Lupercalia

In a completely historically accurate representation of Lupercalian festivities in Rome, a student pretends to be a wolf, seeking to break up pairs of lovers. Here, he is being slapped by our eminent Reges Certaminis in the famous Roman refertilization chamber.

Vergil's Birthday

On this oft-underrated holiday taking place on October 15, we pay homage to one of the greatest curriculum writers for AP Latin who ever lived with a reenactment of a baby's Lustratio, or naming ceremony.
